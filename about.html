About K.S Enterprises

K.S Enterprises is a trusted manufacturer and exporter of premium-quality herbal products based in Murshidabad, West Bengal, India. *Our flagship product is Ashwagandha Root, sourced from renowned cultivation regions of Maharashtra and Rajasthan, including the highly valued Nagori Ashwagandha, which is widely recognized as one of India's finest quality Ashwagandha varieties.* We specialize in the procurement, processing, manufacturing and export of Ashwagandha Root, Ashwagandha Powder, Kapoor Kachri, other herbal products. Our commitment to quality ensures that every product is carefully selected, processed and packed to meet the requirements of domestic and international buyers. Known for its superior potency, high withanolide content and excellent quality, Nagori Ashwagandha is our signature offering and a preferred choice among importers, manufacturers and herbal industries worldwide. At K.S Enterprises, we focus on delivering natural, authentic and premium-grade products while maintaining competitive pricing, reliable supply chains and timely delivery. Our mission is to promote India's finest herbal and jute products in global markets and build long-term partnerships based on trust, quality and professionalism. K.S Enterprises is an India-based export company committed to delivering quality products to domestic and international markets.
